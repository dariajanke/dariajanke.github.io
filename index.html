About this project

Foreign Film Fountain was created by Film Student Daria Janke as a gateway for people interested in international film. Studies have shown that watching foreign films enables people to understand the history and culture of other nations and be more openminded about the world. As such film is a great tool for education and cultural exchange.
To help people decide what film to start with, the list of recommendations is split into genre and additional references. This way, it is easier to find a film one might enjoy based on previous cinematic experience. Additionally, when availiable, we provide links to trailers as well as online platforms where the films can be watched - legally - with english subtitles.
Foreign Film Fountan is at once a source for film recommendations and an online video aggregator, helping people decide what to watch and where to watch it.
